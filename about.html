About Us

Established over 38 years ago, SKJ Business Solutions LLP has been a cornerstone in guiding exporters and importers through the complexities of India’s foreign trade policies and regulatory frameworks. With in-depth experience working closely with DGFT, Customs, ECGC, and FIEO, our firm has successfully catered to a wide range of clients across industries.

We specialize in offering customized solutions for all major export-import schemes such as EPCG, Advance Authorization, DFIA, RoDTEP, RoSCTL, IEC registration, and much more. Our founder has built long-standing relationships at both regional and headquarter levels of DGFT, ensuring a smooth and compliant journey for our clients.

Today, SKJ Business Solutions LLP is evolving under the leadership of the next generation, embracing digital platforms, expanding internationally, and scaling consultancy with a mission to empower Indian exporters. Our focus remains strong on timely delivery, expert documentation, and transparent client service.
