EPCG License Services

The Export Promotion Capital Goods (EPCG) Scheme allows import of capital goods for pre-production, production, and post-production at zero customs duty. It is aimed at enhancing India’s export competitiveness by facilitating modern technology access.

Our EPCG License Services Include:

  • Guidance on EPCG eligibility, documentation & application process
  • Preparation and submission of the complete EPCG License application to DGFT
  • Liaison with DGFT Zonal and Head offices to ensure timely issuance
  • Support for compliance, bond execution, and post-license obligations
  • Redemption and closure assistance after export obligations are fulfilled

We ensure complete handholding and timely updates throughout the lifecycle of your EPCG License. With 38+ years of industry experience, we help minimize delays and maximize your trade benefits.
